CO TOKEN REWARDS

Co token is a reflection token. HODLers automatically receive airdrops directly in their wallets HODLers will receive passive rewards from simply holding the token in their wallet, as every transaction (buy, sell, and transfer) has a 10% fee.

  • 10% transaction fee is distributed as:
    • Locked Liquidity 5% Pancake Swap
    • Rewards 4%
    • Charity 0.5% a wallet
    • Mining Pool 0.5% a wallet
  • Tokenomics
    • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000,000,000
    • Burning (with Automatic Compensation Mechanism) 49%
    • Charity 0.25%
    • Mining Pool 0.25%
    • Contractual Locked Liquidity 25%
    • Community Participants during liquidity drive event 25%
