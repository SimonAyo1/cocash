The treasuries ( Co Token Treasury and Cash Treasury) are very vital in the Cash bonding System. The Co Token treasury is the Co Cash reserve of funds that is used to provide partial collateral for Stable Coin while the Cash Treasury is the BUSD reserve of funds that is used to provide partial collateral for Stable Coin. Since the Co Token Treasury acts as a large player, it always ensures a Collateralization Ratio above 1 using the rewards from incoming trading volume from Co Token. Collateralization Ration is the current asset ratio of the combined treasuries (Cash and Co Token) to the Co Token/BUSD Liquidity Pool (LP) in Pancake Swap. When the ratio exceeds 1 that means there is an excess to pay for the yield and redemption of Stable Coins in the ecosystem.
